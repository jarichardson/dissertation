\chapter{Sill thicknesses in the San Rafael Swell, Utah}\label{apdx_lidar}
Thickness measurements are made for six sills in the San Rafael Swell, Utah study region (Chapter \ref{ch_sills}). Measurements are made in the software Lidar Viewer using areal and terrestrial lidar data. The data is georeferenced to the Universal Transverse Mercator Zone 12N, coordinate system. Thickness is reported in the tables below as the vertical distance between the interpretted top and bottom of sill outcrops at a geographic location.
